About SUSIE Furniture

Craftsmanship rooted in family, material, and memory

SUSIE Furniture began with a simple belief: the right table changes dinner, the right sofa keeps people talking longer, and the right bed makes tomorrow gentler. Every piece is selected for honest materials, balanced proportions, and the kind of comfort that becomes part of daily life.

From solid walnut frames to soft performance fabrics, our showroom brings refined design into homes across the Philippines with care, service, and delivery that respects the moments waiting to happen.
